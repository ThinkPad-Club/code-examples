# Classroom Rules 🫡

We want ThinkPad Club to be **fun and focused** so everyone can learn and build cool things.

## Our Rules

- Wear **headphones**
- Stay **in your seat**
- Use **your own computer**
- Hands **to yourself**
- **No outside games**
- **No outside videos**

These rules help everyone **focus and learn**.

---

## Three Strikes

If a student keeps breaking the rules, they may get a **strike**.

If a student gets **three strikes**, they may need to go back to **aftercare for 15 minutes or longer**, or sometimes for the rest of the club time.

This helps the class stay **quiet and focused**.

Students are always welcome to come back next time and try again.

---

## If You Need a Break

Sometimes everyone needs a break.

You can:

- Take a short walk  
- Get some water  
- Come back ready to work

Then we can keep **learning and building together**.
