# Classroom Conduct 🫡

To make ThinkPad Club productive and enjoyable for everyone, please follow these guidelines.

## Basic Expectations

- **Headphones are mandatory**
- Stay **in your seat**
- Keep **hands on your own computer**
- **No playing games**
- **No watching videos**

These expectations help everyone stay focused and allow the club to run smoothly.

ThinkPad Club is a time for building projects, learning new skills, and helping each other solve problems. When students are distracted or distracting others, it makes it harder for everyone to concentrate and learn. Following these guidelines helps create a calm, productive environment where everyone can do their best work.

---

## Three Strike Rule

Students receive **three strikes** for distracting or unprofessional conduct, including the behaviors listed above.

If a student receives **three strikes**, they may be asked to return to **aftercare for 15 or more minutes**, or in some cases **for the remainder of the session**.

This is not meant as a punishment. It is simply a way to restore a focused learning environment for the rest of the group.

Students are always welcome to return to the club the following session ready to participate and build.

---

## Need a Break?

If you are feeling distracted or frustrated, it’s okay to reset.

You can:

- Take a short walk
- Get some water
- Come back ready to focus

Everyone needs a break sometimes. The goal is always to **get back to learning and building projects.**
